# 📊 Mahadev Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

# 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive E-commerce Sales Dashboard built using Excel and Power BI. It analyzes sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, and product categories to help businesses make data-driven decisions and improve overall performance.

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

# 📂 Dataset
Orders Data and Customer Details Data with Dashboard Document - [Project.zip](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/27338179/Project.zip)

# 🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales, profit, and quantity
Identify top-performing states and customers
Understand category-wise sales distribution
Track monthly profit trends
Analyze payment modes and customer preferences

# 🔍 Key Analysis
Sales Overview: Total Sales (438K), Profit (37K), Quantity (6K)
State-wise Profit: Comparison of profit across different states
Category Analysis: Clothing, Electronics, Furniture contribution
Monthly Trends: Profit variation across months
Customer Insights: Top customers based on purchase
Payment Modes: COD, UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, EMI
Sub-category Performance: Profit by items like T-shirts, Trousers, Tables

# 📈 Insights & Business Impact
Clothing category contributes the highest sales share
COD is the most preferred payment method
Certain states like Uttar Pradesh generate higher profit
Identified top customers driving major revenue
Monthly trends help in planning seasonal sales strategies
